1. COW DODGE: C++ Console Game

Cow Dodge: Alien Invasion is a fast-paced console game developed by Ramzy using C++. In this quirky and action-packed adventure, players take control of a UFO and must dodge herds of cows stampeding across the screen. The game features retro-style ASCII visuals, an intuitive stat system that displays your health, shield, and coins, and fun mechanics like laser blasts and protective shields. With every dodge and zap, players must stay sharp to survive the chaos. It’s a fun, challenging, and creative twist on alien invasion games.
