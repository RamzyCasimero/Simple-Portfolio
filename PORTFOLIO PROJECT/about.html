ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Ramzy, but you can also call me Ram, a student pursuing a degree in Information Technology. I’m 19 years old and I’ve always been fascinated by technology from a young age. Growing up in Tanza, Cavite, I was naturally drawn to computers and coding, which led me to start experimenting with programming languages like C++. Outside of my studies, I am passionate about gaming and building personal coding projects. One of my main projects is my C++ game called 'Cow Dodge: Alien Invasion,' where I apply what I’ve learned in Computer Programming and have fun while doing it. I love the process of turning an idea into something interactive and engaging.

In the future, I hope to combine my passion for technology with creating tools and games that make a real-world impact. I believe that technology can greatly improve everyday lives, whether through entertainment, productivity, or solving real-world problems. As I continue my journey in IT, I’m committed to improving my skills and learning new technologies. I’m excited to contribute to innovative projects in the tech industry and be part of shaping the future of technology.
